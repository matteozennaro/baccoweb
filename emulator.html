Emulators

The bacco emulators provide predictions for cosmological quantities in a wide range of cosmologies, including massive neutrinos and dynamical dark energy.

Our available emulators are:

  • the linear cold matter power spectrum (see Aricò et al, 2021)
  • the nonlinear cold matter power spectrum (see Angulo et al, 2020)
  • the baryonic boost to the nonlinear cold matter power spectrum (see Aricò et al, 2020)
  • [soon to be released] the 2LPT predictions of the 15 cross-spectra that enter the 2nd order Lagrangian bias expansion (see Aricò et al, 2021)
  • [soon to be released] the nonlinear 15 cross-spectra that enter the 2nd order Lagrangian bias expansion (see Zennaro et al, 2021)

The bacco project is under constant development. Emulators get updated as we improve them. Please check out our documentation to have information about installation and usage, our pip page to see the easiest installation way, and our bitbucket public repository to make sure you are up to date with our latest release of the code and the emulators.

These emulators are produced within the bacco project. To build them, we have taken advantage of many techniques developed in our group. In particular, you might be interested in

  • Cosmology rescaling Angulo and White, 2010
  • Extending cosmology rescaling to massive neutrino cosmologies Zennaro et al, 2019
  • Enhancing the accuracy of cosmology rescaling, including massive neutrino and dynamical dark energy scenarios Contreras et al, 2020
  • Including baryonic effects in dark-matter only simulations Aricò et al, 2020
