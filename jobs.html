Open positions

Possibility of hosting MSCA-IF applications

Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions (MSCA) - Individual Fellowships Call 2020 (MSCA-IF-2020) is open. The deadline for application is Sept. 9, 2020 (17:00). The aim of the call is to fund excellent research with bottom-up approach in attractive working conditions, the MSCA is open to experienced researchers of any age, nationality or discipline. The eligibility criteria are as follows: - The researcher needs to have the doctoral degree or at least four years’ full-time research experience at the deadline of submission - The researcher must not have resided or carried out her/his activity in the country of the beneficiary for more than 12 months in the 3 years immediately before call deadline. Do not hesitate to contact us for further information if you would like us to host your application.

One Ph.D. position

The Donostia International Physics Center (DIPC), located in Donostia-San Sebastian, Spain, is currently accepting applications for a PhD position in Cosmology. This PhD position is on the field of cosmological numerical simulations, under the supervision of Prof. Raul Angulo and within the ERC Starting Grant project "BACCO". The successful applicant will work on various aspects related to the large-scale structure of the Universe and the formation of galaxies. Specifically, the PhD will focus on computational modelling of structure in the Universe in the context of upcoming observational campaigns to constrain the nature of dark matter and dark energy. Interested candidates should submit an updated CV and a brief statement of interests to phd@dipc.org with the reference 2020/03 stated in the subject. The applicants should also arrange for 2 two reference letters to be sent to the same email address. Applications received by February 21th will receive full consideration. For more information, please contact Raul Angulo .
